Scrambled Eggs

by Daveon Turner

I usually make this breakfast every other morning at around 8 am of the week. I usually make scrambled eggas because they are easy and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • butter or oil
  • salt
  • pepper

What to Do

  1. Pre-heat pan
  2. Crack Eggs into a bowl
  3. Scramble the eggs with a fork
  4. Place Butter or Oil into pan
  5. Pour mixture into pan
  6. Cook until desired texture
  7. Season to taste
